About Lex Santiago

The Engineer Behind LexMakesIt AI Receptionist

I'm Lex Santiago, a college engineer dedicated to building AI systems that solve real-world problems. My mission is to make advanced technology like AI accessible and beneficial for local service businesses.

Why I Built LexMakesIt

I saw small business owners losing revenue because they couldn't answer the phone while working. I realized that a 24/7 AI-powered receptionist could bridge that gap, handling bookings and inquiries instantly without the cost of a full-time employee.

My Commitment to Privacy

Transparency and data security are the foundation of everything I build. I ensure that all LexMakesIt products follow strict Google API compliance and security best practices.
