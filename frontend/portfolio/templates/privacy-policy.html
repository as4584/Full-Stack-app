Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

Welcome to LexMakesIt AI Receptionist. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI Receptionist services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • OAuth Data: When you connect your Google Calendar, we receive access tokens and basic profile information to provide calendar integration features.
  • Calendar Data: We access your calendar free/busy status and event details strictly for the purpose of checking availability and booking appointments on your behalf.
  • Call Transcripts: We process and store transcripts of calls handled by the AI to provide summaries and service improvements.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our dashboard and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide real-time availability checks and booking for your customers.
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our AI.
  • Secure your account and prevent fraudulent use.
  • Communicate updates or important service notifications.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party services (like OpenAI for processing or Twilio for telephony) as necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure OAuth protocols, to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You can disconnect your Google Calendar at any time through the dashboard, which will revoke our access to your data. You may also request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
